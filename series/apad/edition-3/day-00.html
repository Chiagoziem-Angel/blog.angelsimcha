Welcome to A Proverb A Day — Edition 3

Thirty-one days. One chapter of Proverbs at a time. Straight from the source.

If you're new here, hii! I'm Angel Simcha, and every May and October I run a series called A Proverb A Day — APAD for short.

The premise is simple: the book of Proverbs has 31 chapters. One for every day of the month. So, for 31 days, we read one chapter a day and pull out what it actually means for our lives right now.

I'm a firm believer that the word of God has everything you need for success. Most of the books we read today, every success principle in theory you know of... all came from the Bible. So instead of reading all the derivatives, why not just get it straight from the source?

That's what this series is about. It's about knowing how to apply God's Word for a successful living. Because, just like Peter says...

By his divine power, God has given us everything we need for living a godly life. We have received all of this by coming to know him, the one who called us to himself by means of his marvelous glory and excellence. 2 Peter 1:3 NLT

How it works

Starting tomorrow, a new entry drops every day for 31 days. Each one covers one full chapter of Proverbs — the key themes, what it's saying and how to actually apply it.

You can read along here on the site, or join the telegram channel where they'd be dropped daily.

This is Edition 3. If you were here for Edition 1 (May 2025) or Edition 2 (October 2025)...welcome back. Let's go deeper this time.

If this is your first time... you're right on time. Day 1 starts tomorrow.

Shallom 🤍
