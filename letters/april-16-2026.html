I Choose Your Way

A letter to the Holy Ghost.

April 16th

I choose your way Lord
A letter to Holy Spirit
I choose your way Holy Ghost
It may not be easy
It may not be pleasant to my flesh
But I choose your way Holy Ghost

Make me your dwelling place and reside inside me O Holy Ghost. And help me to always choose your way.

E no easy
This cross is not easy
If you do not help me, I will fall on the road.
You have to help me O Jesus
Please give me the strength to follow you.
Give me the strength to carry my cross and follow you till the end of time.

As I'm treading this holy path, Jesus come and give me strength. I don't want to say yes Lord and at some point I'll say no. I don't want to be hot today and be cold tomorrow. I don't want to please you today and grieve you tomorrow. I want to please you always and make you proud at the end of it all.

Take my will Lord
Take my choice Lord
When it comes to you Yahweh, program me to always say yes
In every situation, in all circumstances
Whether in good or bad, I want to follow you Lord

Teach me to war on my knees and on my feet Lord. And to mount on eagle's wings on my knees Lord.

Help me Lord to walk in your way always. I've chosen your way, now help me Lord walk this walk.

This is my heartcry. Hear me O Lord.

Thank you Lord.
In Jesus name, Amen.

Inspired by Theophilus Sunday — "I Choose Your Way"

Until next time,
Angel
